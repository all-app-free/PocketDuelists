# ⚔️ Pocket Duelists PRO

A fast-paced, 1v1 mobile arena brawler built entirely in JavaScript. Battle against AI, collect trophies, earn coins, and unlock unique "Pockets" (heroes) with special abilities!

## ✨ Features

* **🕹️ Dual Joystick Controls:** Left for movement, Right for aiming and shooting. Optimized for multi-touch.
* **🎯 Quickfire System:** Just tap the right joystick to auto-aim and fire at the nearest enemy.
* **🔥 Super Abilities:** Charge your "Ult" by hitting enemies. Each hero has a unique super power!
* **🌿 Strategic Gameplay:** Hide in **Bushes** to become invisible or hide behind **Walls** for cover.
* **🏆 Trophy Road:** Reach milestones to earn coins and rewards (up to 1000 trophies).
* **🛒 Pockets Shop:** Unlock new heroes like **Colt, Crow, or Leon** using your earned coins.
* **📜 Battle Log:** Keep track of your last 20 matches, victories, and losses.
* **💾 Local Storage:** Your trophies, coins, and unlocked heroes are saved directly on your device.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* **HTML5 Canvas:** For high-performance 2D game rendering.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** Core game logic, physics, and AI (no heavy frameworks needed).
* **CSS3:** Responsive design and mobile orientation handling.
* **GitHub Pages:** Fast and reliable game hosting.

## 📖 How to Play

1.  **Select your Hero:** Go to the **Pockets** menu to choose or buy a hero.
2.  **Start Battle:** Hit the green **BATTLE** button.
3.  **Fight:**
    * Move with the **Left Joystick**.
    * Aim with the **Right Joystick** and release to shoot.
    * Tap the **Right Joystick** for Quickfire.
    * When the **Super button** turns yellow, tap it to unleash your power!
4.  **Win:** Defeat the bot to gain +10 trophies. If you lose, you lose -8 trophies.
